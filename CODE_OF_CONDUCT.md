# AMOS Code of Conduct

AMOS is an open technical platform built by engineers, researchers, and developers from around the world.

We are committed to creating a **respectful and collaborative environment** for everyone involved.

---

# Our Standards

Participants in the AMOS community are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus on constructive technical discussion
- Welcome newcomers and diverse perspectives
- Provide helpful feedback
- Respect differing viewpoints

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or disruptive comments
- Publishing private information
- Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

---

# Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, contributions, or participants that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated violations may result in permanent bans from the community.

---

# Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers.

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

# Our Goal

AMOS exists to advance the development of autonomous mission systems through collaboration, innovation, and shared engineering.

We want this project to be a place where engineers can work together to build something meaningful.
