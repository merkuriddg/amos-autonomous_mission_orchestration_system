⬡ MOS — OPERATOR'S GUIDE

Mission Operating System (MOS) — pronounced "moz" — is the command and control interface for the Mavrix1 Robotic Platoon. This guide covers every control on your C2 console.

📊 PLATOON STATUS

FieldMeaning
TOTALTotal number of connected assets (should be 25)
ACTIVEAssets currently executing a mission or en route
THREATSActive threat contacts detected in the last 30 seconds
AIR / GROUND / SEACount of assets by domain

🎯 MISSION ORDERS

Click a mission button to deploy assets to execute that mission type. The Mission Planner decomposes the mission into tasks and assigns available assets.

ButtonMission TypeAssets Used
ISRIntelligence, Surveillance, ReconnaissanceAIR primary
SECURITYPerimeter security and patrolGROUND primary
PRECISION FXPrecision effects / strikeAIR + GROUND
LOGISTICSResupply and transportGROUND (MULE)
SARSearch and RescueAIR + GROUND
EW/SIGINTElectronic Warfare / Signals IntelligenceAIR + MARITIME

💡 Watch the ACTIVITY LOG for confirmation that the mission was received and tasks were created.

🔄 SWARM COMMANDS

These affect ALL assets simultaneously:

ButtonAction
RTBReturn To Base — all assets navigate back to their starting positions
HOLDAll assets stop in place and hold current position
SCATTEREmergency disperse — all assets move to random nearby positions

🔷 FORMATIONS

Formations reposition assets into geometric patterns. Select a DOMAIN filter first:

FormationPattern
LINEAssets spread in a horizontal line (East-West)
COLUMNAssets form a vertical column (North-South)
WEDGEV-shape with lead asset at front
DIAMONDDiamond/rhombus pattern (best with 4+ assets)
ORBITCircle formation around center point

💡 Set DOMAIN to AIR to only move air assets, etc.

🤖 AUTONOMY LEVELS

Controls how much independence the platoon has. Higher levels require HPL authorization.

LevelNameDescription
0MANUALFull human control — every action requires operator input
1ASSISTEDAssets follow orders with basic obstacle avoidance (default)
2COLLABORATIVEAssets coordinate with each other on tasks
3SWARMFull swarm intelligence — ⚠️ Requires HPL authorization
4COGNITIVEFull autonomous decision-making — ⚠️ Requires HPL authorization

📍 WAYPOINT MODE

Assign custom routes to individual assets:

StepAction
1Click 📍 WP MODE on the map toolbar (turns green when active)
2Click on the map to place waypoints (numbered diamond markers appear)
3Select an asset from the -- assign to -- dropdown
4Check LOOP if you want the asset to repeat the route endlessly
5Click ASSIGN to send the route to that asset
6Repeat steps 3-5 with a different asset to assign the SAME waypoints to multiple assets

💡 Click CLEAR to remove all waypoint markers and start over.

📋 ASSET DETAIL PANEL

Click any asset marker on the map to open the detail panel on the right side:

FieldInfo
Battery barGreen >50%, yellow >20%, red <20%
Comms barSignal strength in dBm
WaypointsShows current/total waypoint progress + loop indicator ↻

Individual asset commands:

ButtonAction
RTBSend this single asset back to its starting position
HOLDStop this asset and cancel its current mission
SEND HEREClick this, then click the map — asset moves to that point
CANCELCancel all orders for this asset (same as HOLD)

MarkerMeaning

● Yellow coreAsset is EN ROUTE to target

◌ Red dashedDetected threat contact

⌨️ TIPS

• Hover over any marker to see a quick tooltip with callsign, status, and battery
• The Activity Log color-codes entries: orange = threats, blue = commands, cyan = waypoints
• Threats auto-expire after 30 seconds if not refreshed by the detection system
• Use SCATTER in emergencies, then RTB to regroup
• Formations use the center-of-mass of the selected domain as the formation center
